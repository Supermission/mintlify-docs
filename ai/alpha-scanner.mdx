# Find the mispriced markets

The Alpha Scanner runs every 30 minutes, comparing the AI's probability estimate against the market price for every active market. When it finds a meaningful divergence, it calculates the expected value, suggests optimal position sizing, and packages the full reasoning chain into an actionable edge card.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI analyzes every market">
    The multi-agent system produces a probability estimate for each active market (tiered by volume and importance).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edge calculated">
    For each market: edge = AI probability minus market probability. A 72% AI estimate vs 45% market price = 27% edge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expected value and sizing computed">
    Expected value per dollar and optimal position size are calculated from the edge and odds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edges ranked and classified">
    Edges are ranked by conviction, edge size, and expected value. Classified as high conviction, moderate, or speculative.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Classification

| Level | Criteria | Meaning |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **High conviction** | Edge 25%+, 3/4+ agents agree, confidence 75+ | Strongest setups — agents agree, evidence is clear |
| **Moderate** | Edge 15%+, 2/4+ agents agree, confidence 50+ | Decent opportunity with some uncertainty |
| **Speculative** | Below moderate thresholds | Interesting but uncertain — smaller position sizes recommended |

## The math

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Expected Value">
    **Expected Value = (AI probability x payout) - (miss probability x cost)**

    Example: AI says 72%, market price 45¢
    - Cost per share: $0.45
    - Payout if right: $1.00
    - EV = (0.72 x $1.00) - (0.28 x $0.45) = $0.72 - $0.126 = **+$0.594 per share**
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Optimal Sizing">
    The scanner calculates the mathematically optimal fraction of your bankroll to allocate based on the edge size and odds.

    Example: AI 72%, Market 45%
    - Optimal fraction: **49%**
    - Capped by your max-per-trade setting (typically much lower)

    In practice, half or quarter of the suggested fraction is safer. The scanner caps sizing at your configured maximum to prevent overexposure.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Most profitable setup: High conviction edges in high-volume markets. The liquidity ensures clean execution, and agent agreement means the evidence is strong. Sort by EV, filter to 75+ confidence.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Edge does not equal guaranteed profit. The AI can systematically misjudge novel situations. Always read the reasoning chain and risk factors before sizing up. The edge card shows you everything you need to form your own view.
</Warning>
