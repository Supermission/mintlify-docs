# Your DeFi research desk

Delphi is the other side of Supermission — separate from prediction markets entirely. It's a chat-based AI agent built for DeFi: token analysis, yield scanning, portfolio bridging, and on-chain intelligence.

If Oracle is your prediction market trader, Delphi is your DeFi analyst.

## What Delphi can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Token Analysis" icon="coins">
    Deep dives on any token — price action, holder distribution, liquidity depth, contract risk. "Analyze AERO token" and Delphi pulls the full picture.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Yield Scanning" icon="seedling">
    Finds the best yield opportunities across protocols. Filters by chain, risk level, and APY. "Find best yields on Base" returns ranked opportunities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Swaps & Bridges" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Execute token swaps and cross-chain bridges through conversation. "Swap 100 USDC to ETH on Base" — Delphi handles routing and execution.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wallet Intel" icon="wallet">
    Analyze any wallet address. Holdings breakdown, transaction history, DeFi positions. Useful for whale-watching outside prediction markets.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why it's separate from Oracle

Oracle is purpose-built for prediction markets — searching, analyzing, and executing trades on Polymarket. Delphi handles everything else in crypto.

Different tools, different specializations, same terminal.

<Info>
  Delphi uses the same wallet infrastructure as the rest of Supermission. Your Base wallet, your funds, same security model. No additional setup required.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use Delphi and Oracle together. Delphi spots a token trend, Oracle checks if there's a prediction market on it, you trade the convergence. That's the alpha loop.
</Tip>
