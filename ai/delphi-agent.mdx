# Delphi Agent

Delphi handles DeFi. Different from Oracle, which focuses on prediction markets.

## What it does

**Token analysis.** Ask about any token. Delphi pulls price data, liquidity, volume, smart money flows, and gives you a breakdown.

**Yield scanning.** Find the best yields across protocols on Base, Ethereum, and Polygon. Filter by risk, APY, or protocol.

**Swaps.** Tell Delphi to swap tokens and it routes through Uniswap. You confirm before execution.

**Wallet intelligence.** Paste any wallet address. Delphi shows you holdings, recent activity, and DeFi positions.

## Examples

"What's trending on Base right now?"

"Analyze the AERO token"

"Find me yields above 10% on Base"

"Swap 50 USDC to ETH"

"What's in this wallet: 0x..."

Go to the DeFi section from the main navigation. Delphi lives there with its own chat interface and session history.
