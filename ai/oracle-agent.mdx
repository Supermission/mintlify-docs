# Oracle Agent

Oracle is a chat agent that trades prediction markets for you.

You type what you want in plain English. It figures out the rest.

## What it can do

Search markets, check your positions, analyze specific markets, place trades, sell positions, look up your watchlist, and research topics on the web.

## Examples

**"What should I bet on?"**
Oracle queries the signal feed for high-conviction opportunities and shows you the top picks with reasoning.

**"Buy $25 on YES for the Bitcoin above 100K market"**
It finds the market, checks the price, calculates shares, and shows you a confirmation card. You tap confirm, trade executes.

**"Sell all my losing positions"**
It pulls your portfolio, identifies positions in the red, and queues sell orders for each one. You confirm each one individually.

**"What's happening with the Fed market?"**
It searches for the market, pulls the latest AI analysis, checks whale positions, and gives you a summary.

**"What's in my portfolio?"**
Shows your open positions with current P&L, sorted by value.

## Good to know

Oracle can research and recommend, but it always asks for confirmation before executing a trade. It won't spend your money without you saying yes.

The agent has access to live market data, your positions, the signal feed, and web search. It doesn't have access to your private keys or wallet directly.
