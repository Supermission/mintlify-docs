# Signal feed

The **Signals** tab in the Markets terminal shows every market where the AI has found a meaningful divergence from the current price. Unlike the AI Pick badges on market cards (which are quick indicators), the signal feed gives you full signal data with rich filtering and sorting.

## Signal card anatomy

Each signal card displays:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Type badge** | Signal type with color: **Sentiment** (purple), **Momentum** (amber), **Spot Price** (cyan) |
| **Market question** | The market being analyzed |
| **Confidence bar** | 0-100% horizontal bar showing AI confidence level |
| **Live price** | Current YES/NO price, updated in real time |
| **AI Pick badge** | Which outcome the AI favors (YES or NO) |
| **Asset tag** | If crypto-related, shows the asset symbol |
| **Trade button** | Opens the market for trading |

## Filters

### Signal type filter

| Type | What it finds |
|------|---------------|
| **ALL** | Every active signal |
| **SENTIMENT** | AI's independent probability based on news, social data, and fundamentals. Fires when AI confidence diverges from market price. |
| **MOMENTUM** | Price action has moved in the direction the AI predicted. The market is confirming the AI's thesis. |
| **SPOT PRICE** | Crypto-specific. A token crosses a threshold relevant to a prediction market (e.g., BTC above $100K triggers related market signals). |

### Category filter

Use the dropdown to narrow signals to a specific market category — Politics, Crypto, Sports, Finance, and more.

### Info button

Tap the "?" icon for a quick explainer on each signal type if you need a refresher.

## Sorting

| Sort | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| **Confidence** (default) | Highest AI confidence first. The signals the AI is most sure about. |
| **Volume** | Highest market volume first. Liquid markets where execution is cleanest. |
| **Ending Soon** | Closest to resolution first. Time-sensitive opportunities you don't want to miss. |
| **Price Change** | Biggest 24h movers first. Where the action is right now. |

## Browsing signals

The feed loads more results as you scroll. The total number of active signals is displayed at the top so you always know how many opportunities are available.
