# AI War Room

Every prediction market tool shows you a probability. We show you the argument behind it.

The War Room runs four agents on a market. Each one analyzes independently. They disagree. A judge picks the strongest reasoning and produces a final view. You see everything.

## The four agents

**Bull** looks for reasons the YES price should be higher. Catalysts, positive sentiment, underpriced scenarios.

**Bear** does the opposite. Risk factors, negative data, things the market might be ignoring.

**Contrarian** asks "what if everyone is wrong?" Crowded trades, false consensus, edge cases nobody is pricing.

**Quant** skips the narratives. Volume patterns, historical base rates, orderbook depth, time decay.

## What shows up

**Edge Calculator.** The gap between what our AI thinks and what the market says. If the AI says 72% and the market says 55%, that's a 17-point edge. Bigger gap means bigger potential opportunity. Also means the AI might just be wrong.

**Conviction Meter.** How much the four agents agree. When all four line up, the meter fills. When they're split, it shows that too.

**Key Risk.** One sentence. The single biggest thing that could make this trade go sideways. If you only read one thing in the War Room, read this.

**Accuracy Scoreboard.** How often the AI has been right on similar markets. Broken down by category. If it says 80% confidence and historically hits 78% at that level, the calibration is solid.

## How to open it

Click any market card. In the detail panel, tap the **AI** tab. That's it.

<Tip>
  Pay extra attention when the Contrarian agrees with Bull or Bear. It means even the "what if everyone is wrong" agent couldn't find a counterargument.
</Tip>
