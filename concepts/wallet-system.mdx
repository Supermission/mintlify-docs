# Your wallet

When you sign up for Supermission, a wallet is created for you automatically. No seed phrases to write down. No browser extensions to install. No gas fees to worry about.

Your funds are yours — we literally cannot access them. You sign in with your email, and your wallet is there.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="You sign up">
    Use your email, Google, or connect an existing wallet like MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet created instantly">
    A secure wallet is generated for you on sign-up. Your private key is protected using advanced split-key security — no single party (including us) ever has full access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You sign in, wallet's ready">
    Every time you log in, your wallet is securely reconstructed. You never see or manage a private key. It just works.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You stay in control">
    Every trade is signed by your wallet. We cannot execute transactions without your authentication. Period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What this means for you

| Benefit | What it means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Your money, your control** | We never hold your funds. We can't move your money, freeze your account, or access your wallet. Technically can't — not "promise we won't." |
| **No seed phrases** | You don't need to memorize or store 24 random words. If you lose your phone, just log back in with your email. |
| **Zero gas fees** | Every transaction fee is covered by us. You never need to buy ETH or worry about gas. Trades, swaps, bridges — all free for you. |
| **No chain switching** | Your wallet handles multiple chains behind the scenes. When you trade prediction markets, bridge tokens, or deposit into DeFi — the routing happens automatically. You don't even notice. |
| **Batch transactions** | Multiple operations (like approve + swap) can happen in a single click. Less waiting, less friction. |

## How trading works behind the scenes

You confirm a trade. Your wallet signs it. The transaction goes through. That's all you need to know.

Behind the scenes, your USDC gets routed to wherever it needs to be. If you're trading prediction markets, the bridging happens automatically. When you sell or win, the funds come back to your wallet. You see step 1 (confirm the trade) and the last step (money in your wallet). Everything in between is invisible.

## Connecting an existing wallet

Already have a MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect wallet? You can connect it to your Supermission account. You can use either your Supermission wallet or your connected wallet for trading — and link multiple wallets to one account.

<Warning>
  Your wallet is truly yours. If you lose access to your email or social login and can't recover it, we cannot help you get back into your wallet. Make sure your login credentials are secure.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  The best wallet experience is one you don't notice. If you're thinking about private keys, gas fees, or which chain you're on while trading — something has gone wrong. We designed Supermission so you never have to.
</Tip>
