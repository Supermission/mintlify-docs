# Your portfolio command center

The Dashboard gives you a complete picture of your financial life across every chain and protocol. Token balances with price charts, DeFi positions you can manage directly, yield opportunities ranked by risk and return, and AI that analyzes your whole portfolio.

No more switching between five apps to figure out what you own.

## What you'll find

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Wallet & Tokens" icon="wallet" href="/dashboard/wallet-portfolio">
    See every token you hold across every chain. Live prices, mini charts showing recent price action, and total portfolio value. Add wallets you want to watch (even if they're not yours).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Token Search" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/dashboard/token-search">
    Look up any token. Get price, market cap, volume, and — for premium users — a full AI-powered due diligence report with a BUY/HOLD/SELL verdict.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DeFi Positions" icon="landmark" href="/dashboard/defi-positions">
    Manage your lending deposits and liquidity positions. See what you've earned, collect fees, add or remove liquidity — all without leaving Supermission.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Yield Strategies" icon="seedling" href="/dashboard/yield-strategies">
    Tell the AI what you want (e.g., "safe yield on USDC" or "aggressive growth") and it recommends specific vaults and pools. Deposit in one click.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

  You need to be logged in to access the Dashboard. If you haven't already, [create your account](/getting-started/create-account) first.
