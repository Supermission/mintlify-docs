# Generate images from a prompt

The DeFAI chat supports AI-powered image generation. Describe what you want, pick your options, and get a result in seconds. Then refine it with variations, style changes, and quality upgrades.

## How to use

Type directly in the chat:
- `"Generate a cyberpunk city at night"`
- `"Create an image of a futuristic trading terminal"`
- `"Draw a logo concept for a crypto project"`

## Options

Before generation, you can choose:

| Option | Choices |
|--------|---------|
| **Quality tier** | Flash (cheapest, fastest), Standard, Premium (highest quality) — each shows price in USDC |
| **Aspect ratio** | 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 4:3, 3:4 |
| **Style preset** | Auto, Vibrant, Muted, Cinematic, B&W, Anime, Neon, Vintage |
| **Background** | Auto, Transparent, White, Blur |

## Results

Your generated image appears with:
- **Cost badge** showing USDC spent
- **Download button** to save the image
- **Info panel** (collapsible) with image size and detected style

## Customization

After generation, three action cards let you refine:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Variation" icon="clone">
    Choose variation intensity: Similar (small changes), Different (moderate changes), Extreme (major reimagining). Each triggers a new generation with payment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Style" icon="palette">
    Pick a new style from the same 8 presets (Vibrant, Muted, Cinematic, B&W, Anime, Neon, Vintage, Auto). Applies the style to the existing image concept.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quality" icon="sparkles">
    Upgrade or change the quality tier (Flash/Standard/Premium). Higher tiers produce more detailed, higher-resolution images.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You can also apply pre-built modification presets (Vibrant, Neon, Vintage, Anime, Cinematic, Enhance, B&W) or type a custom prompt for specific modifications like "make the sky more dramatic" or "add neon lighting."

## Pricing

Image generation costs vary by quality tier:
- **Flash** — ~$0.08, fastest generation, good for quick concepts
- **Standard** — mid-range quality and price, solid for most uses
- **Premium** — highest quality and resolution, best for final outputs

Costs are always shown before you confirm, so there are no surprises.
