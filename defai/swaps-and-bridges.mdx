# Swaps and bridges

Ask any DeFAI agent to swap tokens or bridge assets, and it prepares an inline execution panel. Routing is handled automatically, aggregating DEX liquidity across 35+ chains to find you the best price.

## How to use

- `"Swap 100 USDC to ETH on Base"`
- `"Bridge 500 USDC from Ethereum to Base"`
- `"How much ETH can I get for 1000 USDC?"`

## Swap flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Agent prepares quote">
    The agent finds the best route, considering slippage, gas costs, and liquidity depth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quote displays">
    You see:
    - **From token and To token** with amounts
    - **Price impact** percentage
    - **Gas estimate**
    - **Route details**
    - Suggestion chips for follow-up actions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm execution">
    Review the exact amounts, price impact, gas estimate, and route path. Click **Confirm** to execute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Multi-step progress">
    The execution follows these steps:
    1. **Check** — verify your balance and network
    2. **Approve** — token approval (if needed, first time only)
    3. **Execute** — swap transaction
    4. **Done** — confirmation with transaction link

    Each step shows a checkmark (complete), spinner (in progress), or dimmed (pending).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    On success or failure, the agent sends a confirmation message. Your wallet balances update automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cross-chain bridges

Cross-chain transfers use the same conversational flow with automatic bridging:

- **USDC transfers** between Base, Ethereum, Polygon, and Arbitrum use automatic bridging — native USDC with no wrapped tokens or intermediaries.
- **Other tokens** route through aggregated bridge paths across 35+ chains.

The inline panel shows the source chain, destination chain, and estimated transfer time.

## Tips for better swaps

- **Check price impact** before confirming large swaps. High impact means you're moving the market against yourself.
- **Bridge USDC first** if you need tokens on another chain. Swap USDC to your target token on the destination chain for better rates.
- **Use quotes to compare.** Ask "How much ETH can I get for 1000 USDC?" before committing. The agent shows you the rate without executing.

<Tip>
  For USDC transfers between major chains, automatic bridging is the most efficient route — native USDC with no wrapped intermediaries. The system automatically selects the best path for you.
</Tip>
