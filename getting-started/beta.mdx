# Supermission Beta

**Be early. Trade smarter. One terminal.**

## Welcome to the Supermission Beta

The doors are open — but only for a select group of traders ready to test what could become the most powerful AI-powered prediction market terminal in crypto.

The beta is live with **4,500+ prediction markets**, powered by AI agents that deliver real-time alpha from multi-agent debates, whale tracking, and on-chain signal detection. DeFAI agents for token research, contract audits, yield hunting, and cross-chain execution are all included.

More markets, more agents, and more features are shipping every week.

## Who can join

We're inviting sharp traders, prediction market enthusiasts, and DeFi power users to join the Supermission beta. You'll help us stress-test the terminal and share feedback that shapes the final product.

This is invite-only. If you're reading this, you're probably already in — or close.

## Beta rewards

- **Trading fee rebates** during the beta period — trade more, keep more
- **Exclusive early-adopter perks** as a thank-you for building with us
- **Leaderboard incentives** — the more you trade, the more you earn (coming soon)
- **First access** to new AI agents and features before anyone else
