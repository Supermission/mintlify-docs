# Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to supermission.fun and click Get Started" />
  <Step title="Sign in with email, Google, or an existing wallet">
    Privy handles authentication. Pick whatever is fastest for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your wallet is created automatically">
    A smart wallet on Base. No seed phrases to write down, no extensions to install.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Fund your wallet with USDC and start trading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The whole thing takes about 30 seconds.
