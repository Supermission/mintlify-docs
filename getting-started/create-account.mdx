# Account setup — 30 seconds, no seed phrases

Supermission lets you sign in like a normal app — email, Google, or connect an existing wallet. No MetaMask popups. No 12-word backup rituals.

A smart wallet gets created for you automatically. It's yours. You control it. We just made the UX not terrible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to supermission.fun">
    Hit [supermission.fun](https://supermission.fun) and click **Launch Terminal** in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your login method">
    Pick one:
    - **Email** — enter your email, verify with a code
    - **Google** — one-click OAuth, fastest option
    - **Existing wallet** — connect MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect

    All three create the same type of account. Pick whatever's fastest for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart wallet created automatically">
    Once authenticated, a smart wallet is generated for you. This happens in the background — you don't need to do anything. Your wallet address appears in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    That's it. You're looking at the terminal. Browse markets, check AI signals, or [fund your wallet](/getting-started/fund-wallet) to start trading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your smart wallet is an on-chain account. It's non-custodial — we never hold your keys. Key management is handled with hardware-level security behind the scenes.
</Info>

## What's a smart wallet?

It's an on-chain account that acts like a regular wallet but with better UX:

- **No gas management** — transactions are sponsored, you never pay gas
- **No seed phrases** — key recovery is handled through your login method
- **No chain switching** — we bridge assets behind the scenes when needed
- **Batched transactions** — multiple operations can happen in a single click

<Warning>
  While your wallet is non-custodial, Supermission is in beta. Don't deposit more than you're actively trading with.
</Warning>
