# Account setup — 30 seconds, no seed phrases

Supermission uses [Privy](https://privy.io) for authentication. That means you sign in like a normal app — email, Google, or connect an existing wallet. No MetaMask popups. No 12-word backup rituals.

A smart wallet gets created for you automatically on Base. It's yours. You control it. We just made the UX not terrible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to supermission.fun">
    Open your browser and navigate to [supermission.fun](https://supermission.fun).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Launch Terminal">
    Find the **Launch Terminal** button in the top-right corner of the landing page. Click it to open the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your login method">
    A login modal will appear. Pick one:
    - **Email** — enter your email address and verify with a one-time code sent to your inbox
    - **Google** — one-click OAuth sign-in, fastest option
    - **Existing wallet** — connect MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect

    All three create the same type of account. Pick whatever's fastest for you.

    <img src="/images/login.png" alt="Login modal" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    If you chose email, check your inbox for a verification code and enter it. If you chose Google, approve the OAuth prompt. If you connected a wallet, sign the message in your wallet app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart wallet created automatically">
    Once authenticated, a smart wallet is generated for you on Base. This happens in the background — you don't need to do anything. Your wallet address appears in the top-right corner of the terminal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    That's it. You're now looking at the terminal. Browse markets, check AI signals, or [fund your wallet](/getting-started/fund-wallet) to start trading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your smart wallet is an on-chain account on Base. It's non-custodial — we never hold your keys. Privy's embedded wallet infrastructure handles key management with hardware-level security.
</Info>

## What's a smart wallet?

It's an on-chain account that acts like a regular wallet but with better UX:

- **No gas management** — transactions are sponsored, you never pay gas
- **No seed phrases** — key recovery is handled through your login method
- **No chain switching** — we bridge assets behind the scenes when needed
- **Batched transactions** — multiple operations can happen in a single click

<Warning>
  While your wallet is non-custodial, Supermission is in beta. Don't deposit more than you're actively trading with.
</Warning>
