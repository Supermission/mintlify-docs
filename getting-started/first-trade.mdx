# Your first trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse markets">
    Open the Markets tab. Scroll through or search for something interesting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click a market card">
    The detail panel opens on the right. You'll see prices, volume, and the probability bars.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a side">
    Hit YES or NO. The amount picker appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how much">
    Tap a preset ($1, $5, $10, $25, $50, $100) or type a custom amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    You'll see potential profit if correct, potential loss if wrong, and estimated shares. Hit Confirm Trade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Trade executes in a few seconds. Your position shows up in the Portfolio tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After the trade, three things can happen. The market keeps trading and your position changes value. The market resolves and you win (shares pay $1 each). Or the market resolves and you lose (shares pay $0). You can also sell before resolution at the current market price.
