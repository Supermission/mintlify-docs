# Fund your wallet

Your wallet address is in the top-right corner of the screen. Click it to copy.

Send USDC on Base to that address. From Coinbase, Binance, or any wallet that supports Base.

That's it. Your balance updates in a few seconds.

## What happens when you trade

When you place a trade, Supermission automatically bridges your USDC from Base to Polygon (where Polymarket's orderbook lives). You don't see this. It just happens.

Gas fees are covered by the platform. You never need ETH.

<Tip>
  If you already have USDC on Polygon, that works too. The system detects balances on both chains and uses whatever is available.
</Tip>
