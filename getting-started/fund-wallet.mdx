# Get USDC into your wallet

Supermission runs on USDC. Your wallet lives on Base. When you trade, we automatically bridge USDC to Polygon where Polymarket's orderbook lives. You don't need to think about chains.

<img src="/images/funds.png" alt="Wallet panel showing your USDC balance" />

## Fund your wallet step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the wallet panel">
    Click the **wallet icon** in the top-right corner of the terminal. This opens the wallet panel where you can see your balance and wallet address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your wallet address">
    Click the wallet address (starts with `0x`) to copy it to your clipboard. This is a standard Base wallet address — any USDC sent here will show up in your balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to your exchange or wallet">
    Open the platform you're sending USDC from. Common sources:
    - **Coinbase** — go to Send/Withdraw, search for USDC
    - **Binance** — go to Withdraw, search for USDC
    - **Another wallet** (e.g. MetaMask, Rainbow) — tap Send
    - **Bridge** — use [Across](https://across.to) to move USDC from Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Optimism to Base
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Base network">
    This is the most important step. When choosing the network, make sure you select **Base**. Sending USDC on the wrong network (e.g. Ethereum, Polygon) means your funds won't appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your wallet address">
    Paste the `0x` address you copied in Step 2 into the recipient/destination field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount and confirm">
    Enter the amount of USDC you want to send. Double-check the network says **Base** and the address matches, then confirm the transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
    Base transactions confirm in under 10 seconds. Once confirmed, your balance updates automatically in the wallet panel.

    <img src="/images/add-funds.png" alt="Add funds confirmation" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're ready to trade">
    Your USDC is now in your wallet. Head to any market and place a trade — we handle bridging to Polygon automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Send **USDC on Base** only. Sending other tokens or USDC on the wrong network means your funds won't appear. Double-check the network before confirming.
</Warning>

## Auto-bridging — how it works

When you place a trade, Supermission bridges your USDC from Base to Polygon automatically. Polymarket uses USDC.e (bridged USDC) on Polygon for its orderbook. Here's what happens behind the scenes:

1. You click "Buy YES" on a market
2. We bridge the exact amount of USDC from Base → Polygon
3. USDC converts to USDC.e on Polygon
4. Trade executes against Polymarket's CLOB
5. Your position shows up in the Portfolio tab

You see step 1 and step 5. Everything in between is invisible.

<Tip>
  Gas fees are fully sponsored. You pay zero gas for trades, bridging, or any on-chain operations. The only cost is the trade itself.
</Tip>

## How much should you deposit?

Start small. Prediction markets are volatile near resolution and there's real risk of losing your entire position. Deposit what you'd be comfortable losing while you learn the mechanics.

<Info>
  Withdrawals are always available. You can send USDC back out to any wallet at any time from the wallet panel.
</Info>
