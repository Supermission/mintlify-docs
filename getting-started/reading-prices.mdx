# Reading prices

Every market card shows a few numbers. Here's what they mean.

**YES / NO price.** The current cost per share. YES at 65 cents means the market thinks there's a ~65% chance. YES + NO prices always add up to roughly $1.

**Volume.** Total money traded in this market over its lifetime. Higher volume generally means more reliable prices.

**24H volume.** Money traded in the last 24 hours. Shows current activity level.

**Countdown.** Time until the market closes. When it hits zero, the market resolves.

**Probability bars.** Visual representation of YES vs NO prices. Green bar is YES, red bar is NO. Proportional to their prices.

## Reading momentum

If a market went from 50 cents to 70 cents in 24 hours, something happened. Check the AI signals tab for what the agents think is driving the move.

Price changes of 5%+ in 24 hours trigger momentum signals in the Signal Feed.
