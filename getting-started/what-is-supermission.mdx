# Bloomberg Terminal for prediction markets

Polymarket has `$44B+` in lifetime volume and 4,500+ active markets. It's the deepest prediction market liquidity on the planet. But trading it feels like using Bloomberg on a Nokia.

Supermission fixes that. It's a terminal — not another market, not another exchange, not another dashboard. We sit on top of Polymarket and give you AI-powered tools to actually trade well.

## How it works, simply

You sign up with your email. A smart wallet gets created on Base. You deposit USDC. When you trade, we bridge to Polygon and execute on Polymarket's orderbook — all invisible to you.

The AI agents run 24/7 analyzing markets. When they spot an edge (their estimated probability vs. market price), it surfaces as a signal. You review the reasoning, decide if you agree, and execute — either by clicking or by chatting with Oracle.

<Tip>
  Think of it this way: Polymarket is the exchange. Supermission is your trading desk.
</Tip>
