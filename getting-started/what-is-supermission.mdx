# What is Supermission?

Supermission is a trading terminal for prediction markets.

Polymarket has 4,500+ markets and $44B in volume. Great liquidity. But the interface gives you prices and leaves the rest to you. No research tools, no signals, no way to understand why a market is moving.

We built what was missing. A terminal that sits on top of Polymarket and gives you tools to actually trade well.

## What you can do

**Trade fast.** Market orders execute instantly. Limit orders let you set your price. Or just tell the Oracle agent what you want in plain English and it handles execution.

**See what the AI thinks.** Four agents independently analyze every market. They look at news, social sentiment, on-chain activity. They disagree with each other. You see the full debate, not just a number.

**Track everything.** Portfolio with live P&L. Watchlist with price alerts. Trade history with CSV export. Position cards sorted by what's ending soonest.

**Know what whales are doing.** Every market shows who the biggest holders are, which side they're on, and how concentrated the positions are.

## How it actually works

You sign up with your email. We create a smart wallet for you on Base. You send USDC to it.

When you trade, we bridge your USDC to Polygon and execute on Polymarket's orderbook. You don't see any of that. You just click and trade.

The AI runs in the background, checking news feeds, Reddit, Bluesky, and market data every 15 minutes. When it finds something interesting, it shows up as a signal.

  Polymarket is the exchange. Supermission is the trading desk you put on top of it.
