## What makes Supermission different

Most prediction market tools are dashboards. You stare at numbers, maybe get an alert, then manually click through 5 screens to place a trade.

Supermission is a **terminal**. The AI does the research. You make the call. The agent executes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI researches markets 24/7">
    4 AI agents independently analyze every market using news, social sentiment, and on-chain data. They debate. A judge synthesizes. You see the reasoning, not just a number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signals surface the alpha">
    When the AI disagrees with the market — that's your edge. We show you exactly where our confidence diverges from the crowd, ranked by opportunity size and conviction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One conversation to trade">
    Tell Oracle "buy $50 on YES for the Fed market" and it's done. No clicking through menus. No switching chains. No managing gas.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The stack

Built on **Base** + **Polygon**. Trades execute on Polymarket's orderbook — the deepest liquidity in prediction markets.

| Layer | Technology | Purpose |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Frontend** | Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript | App Router, React Server Components, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion |
| **Wallet** | Privy MPC | Non-custodial smart wallets with email auth, sponsored gas |
| **Trading** | Polymarket CLOB | Direct orderbook execution, FAK and GTC orders |
| **AI** | OpenRouter, Vercel AI SDK | 4-agent debate system, streaming responses, tool calling |
| **Data** | Supabase PostgreSQL | Market catalog, signals, positions, sessions |
| **Bridging** | Circle CCTP | Automatic USDC bridging between Base and Polygon |
| **DeFi** | Uniswap V3, Morpho, LI.FI | Swaps, LP management, lending, cross-chain routing |
