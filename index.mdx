Supermission is what happens when you give AI agents access to 4,500+ prediction markets and tell them to find alpha.

Four independent AI agents research every market — debating news, sentiment, on-chain data, and historical patterns. A judge synthesizes their arguments. You see the full reasoning, not just a number. When our AI disagrees with the market price, that's your edge.

We built the terminal we wanted as traders: real-time AI signals, one-click execution, whale tracking, and agents that actually understand market dynamics. All on top of the deepest prediction market liquidity in the world — $44B+ in volume and counting.

## What makes Supermission different

Most prediction market tools are dashboards. You stare at numbers, maybe get an alert, then manually click through 5 screens to place a trade.

Supermission is a **terminal**. The AI does the research. You make the call. The agent executes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI researches markets 24/7">
    Four AI agents — Bull, Bear, Contrarian, Quant — independently analyze every market. They look at news, social sentiment, historical patterns, and price action. They debate. A judge synthesizes. You see the full reasoning chain, not just a price target.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signals surface the alpha">
    When the AI disagrees with the market price, that's your opportunity. We show you exactly where our confidence diverges from the crowd — ranked by how big the edge is and how sure we are about it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One conversation to trade">
    Tell Oracle "buy $50 on YES for the Fed market" and it's done. No clicking through menus. No switching apps. No worrying about fees. Just say what you want.
  </Step>
</Steps>
