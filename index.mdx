## What makes this different

Most prediction market tools are dashboards. You stare at numbers, maybe get an alert, then manually click through 5 screens to place a trade.

Supermission is a **terminal**. The AI does the research. You make the call. The agent executes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI researches markets 24/7">
    4 AI agents independently analyze every market using news, social sentiment, and on-chain data. They debate. A judge synthesizes. You see the reasoning, not just a number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signals surface the alpha">
    When the AI disagrees with the market — that's your edge. We show you exactly where our confidence diverges from the crowd, ranked by opportunity size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One conversation to trade">
    Tell Oracle "buy $50 on YES for the Fed market" and it's done. No clicking through menus. No switching chains. No managing gas.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The stack

Built on Base + Polygon. Trades execute on Polymarket's orderbook (deepest liquidity in prediction markets). Your wallet is a Privy-managed smart account — no seed phrases, no gas management, email login.
