## How it works

Most prediction market tools show you numbers and hope you figure it out.

We run four independent AI agents on every market. They analyze news, sentiment, and data. They argue with each other. A judge picks the winner. You see all of it, not just a confidence score.

When the AI thinks the market is wrong, that shows up as a signal. You review the reasoning. If you agree, you trade. If you don't, you move on.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Agents analyze">
    Four agents look at every market from different angles. Bull, Bear, Contrarian, Quant. They disagree on purpose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signals surface">
    When the AI's probability is different from the market price, that's your edge. We rank these by size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You trade">
    Click a button or tell Oracle "buy $50 on YES." It handles the rest. No chain switching, no gas.
  </Step>
</Steps>
