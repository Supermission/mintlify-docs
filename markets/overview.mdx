# Your markets terminal

This is the heart of Supermission. One page. Five tabs. Everything you need to research, trade, and manage prediction markets.

Prices update live — no refreshing needed. You can browse thousands of markets without any lag. And you never have to leave the page to place a trade.

## Terminal layout

The terminal has three sections:

1. **Category sidebar** (left) — filter by category, search for specific markets, sort by what matters to you
2. **Market grid** (center) — all your markets in a clean, scrollable grid
3. **Detail panel** (right) — click any market to open the full analysis view with charts, AI signals, and trading

## Five tabs

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Browse">
    The default view. Every active market, displayed as cards you can scan quickly.

    Each market card shows you:
    - **The question** — what you're betting on
    - **Category badges** — politics, crypto, sports, etc.
    - **Volume** — how much money is flowing through this market
    - **Time remaining** — color-coded so you know what's ending soon (red = less than 1 hour, amber = less than 24 hours)
    - **YES and NO prices** — the current odds, updated live
    - **AI Pick badge** — green for bullish, red for bearish, gray for neutral. This is where our AI thinks the market is mispriced.
    - **Bookmark button** — save markets to your watchlist

    **Trade right from the card:** Click YES or NO on any card. An amount picker slides in with presets ($1, $5, $10, $25, $50, $100 or custom). You see your potential profit before you confirm. One click and you're in.

    More markets load automatically as you scroll. No pagination to click through.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Agent">
    Oracle is your AI trading assistant, built right into the Markets page. Talk to it like a person:

    - "What markets are there about the Fed?"
    - "How's my portfolio doing?"
    - "Buy $25 on YES for Bitcoin above 100k"
    - "Sell my Trump position"

    Oracle always confirms before spending your money. You see the market name, current price, share count, and total cost before anything happens. See the full [Oracle Agent](/ai/oracle-agent) docs.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Signals">
    This is where the AI earns its keep. The signal feed shows you markets where our AI thinks the price is wrong.

    Each signal card shows:
    - **Signal type** — Sentiment (purple), Momentum (amber), or Spot Price (cyan)
    - **The market question**
    - **Confidence** — how sure the AI is (0-100%)
    - **Live price + AI pick** — what the market says vs. what the AI says

    Filter by signal type, category, or minimum confidence. Sort by confidence, volume, ending soon, or price change. See the full [Signal Feed](/ai/signal-feed) docs.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Portfolio">
    Your active positions, open orders, and complete trade history — all in one place.

    Each position shows: what you own, your entry price vs. current price, your P&L in dollars and percent, how many shares you hold, and buttons to sell or redeem.

    Resolved markets show green badges when you've won. Markets ending within 24 hours show amber warnings. See [Portfolio](/trading/portfolio) for details.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Watchlist">
    Markets you're keeping an eye on. Bookmark any market from the Browse tab and it shows up here.

    You can sort by date added, volume, ending soon, or price change. Each watchlisted market has quick-trade buttons so you can jump in without switching tabs. See [Watchlist](/trading/watchlist) for details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
