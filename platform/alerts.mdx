# Price Alerts

Add a market to your watchlist. If it moves more than 5% from your baseline, you get a notification.

The alert slides in from the top-right corner. Click it to jump straight to that market. Swipe right to dismiss. It auto-disappears after 5 seconds.

Different sounds for different directions. Price goes up, one sound. Price goes down, another. You'll learn to tell them apart.

<Warning>
  Alerts only fire for watchlisted markets. If you want notifications on a market, bookmark it first.
</Warning>
