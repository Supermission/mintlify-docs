# Mobile

Supermission is fully responsive. Same features on your phone as on desktop.

Cards stack to single column on small screens. The chat agents work the same. Portfolio, watchlist, signals, all there.

Bottom navigation bar switches between Markets, DeFi, Security, Dashboard, and Blog. It slides away when you scroll down and comes back when you scroll up.

For the best experience, add Supermission to your home screen. Open the site in your mobile browser, tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen." It runs like a native app.
