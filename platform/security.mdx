# Security

Your wallet is a Privy smart account. Non-custodial. We never have access to your private keys.

When you sign up, Privy creates a wallet using multi-party computation (MPC). The key material is split across multiple parties. No single entity, including us, can reconstruct your full key.

Every trade requires your wallet's signature before execution. Gas fees are sponsored by the platform, so you never need ETH for transactions.

The Securo agent can audit any smart contract before you interact with it. Paste an address, get a security report.

<Warning>
  Non-custodial means non-custodial. If you lose access to your email and authentication method, we cannot recover your wallet. Keep your login credentials safe.
</Warning>
