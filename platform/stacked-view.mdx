# Stacked View

In the Browse sidebar, there's a toggle: Normal and Stacked.

Normal shows every market as its own card. Standard grid.

Stacked groups markets by their parent event. An election with 12 sub-markets becomes one card with a dropdown. A sports event with 8 outcomes collapses into a single row.

Click any sub-market in the stack to open it and trade.

<Info>
  Only events with 2+ markets show up in Stacked view. Single-market events don't appear here. Switch to Normal to see everything.
</Info>

## When to use which

**Normal** when you're browsing or searching for something specific. Every market visible, sortable, filterable.

**Stacked** when you want to see the big picture. Which events have the most action. Where the related markets cluster.
