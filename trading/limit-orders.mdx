# Limit orders

Instead of buying at whatever the market offers, you set the exact price you want.

Open a market detail panel. Switch to the **Limit** tab. Pick your outcome (YES/NO), enter your price in cents, enter the number of shares, and submit.

Your order sits on the orderbook until someone matches it or you cancel. Good-Till-Cancel by default.

## When to use limits

When the current price isn't great but you think it'll come to you. When you want to buy at 45 cents but the market is at 50. When you're patient and want better fills.

## Tick sizes

Prices move in increments (tick sizes). Most markets use 1-cent ticks. Some use 0.1-cent ticks. The form shows you the tick size for each market.

## Minimum size

5 shares minimum per limit order.
