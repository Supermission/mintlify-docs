# Market orders

Click a market card. Hit YES or NO. Pick your amount. Confirm. Done.

Your order fills immediately at the best available price on Polymarket's orderbook. This is a Fill-And-Kill order. Whatever doesn't fill instantly gets cancelled.

Good for when you want in now and don't care about saving a penny on the fill price.

<Tip>
  You can also tell Oracle "buy $25 on YES for [market name]" and it handles everything.
</Tip>
