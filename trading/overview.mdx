# Trading on Supermission

Every trade on Supermission executes against the deepest prediction market liquidity on the planet — $44B+ in lifetime volume. Same prices, same depth. Your orders go directly to the orderbook with no front-running risk.

## How a trade works

When you confirm a trade, here's what happens behind the scenes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Balance check">
    The system checks your USDC balance. If you don't have enough, you'll see a warning before anything happens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet preparation">
    Your wallet is automatically set up for the trade. Gas fees are covered — you never pay network fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="USDC approval">
    If this is your first trade, a one-time approval lets the trading contracts move USDC on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Price protection">
    The system fetches the best available price and applies slippage protection (2.5% tolerance) so you don't get a bad fill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order submission">
    Your order hits the orderbook. Instant orders fill immediately. Limit orders sit on the book until someone takes the other side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Position update">
    Your position appears in the Portfolio tab right away. No waiting around.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to trade

You can place trades from three places:

| Location | Method |
|----------|--------|
| **Market card** (Browse tab) | Click YES/NO, pick your amount, confirm. Fastest for quick entries. |
| **Market detail panel** | Click a card to open the side panel with full analysis, then choose instant or limit order. Best for informed trading. |
| **Oracle agent** (Agent tab) | Just say "Buy $50 on YES for the Fed market." Oracle confirms details, then executes. Best for mobile and speed. |
