# Portfolio

The Portfolio tab shows everything about your trading account.

## Top card

Total portfolio value and P&L. Green gradient when you're up, red when you're down. Win rate, position count, total trades, and volume from your trade history.

## Position cards

Each open position gets a card. Shows the market question, your side (YES/NO), entry price, current price, P&L in dollars and percent, and time remaining.

Sorted by ending soonest. Redeemable positions float to the top.

## Open orders

If you have limit orders waiting to fill, they show here with their status and fill percentage.

## Trade history

Scrollable list of all your past trades at the bottom. BUY/SELL badge, market name, amount, and date. Export to CSV if you need it for records.
