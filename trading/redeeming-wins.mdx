# Redeeming winnings

When a market resolves and you held the winning outcome, your shares are worth $1 each.

A **Redeem Winnings** button appears on your position card in the Portfolio tab. Click it. Your shares convert to USDC and bridge back to your Base wallet.

That's it. Takes a few seconds.

## If you held the losing side

Your shares are worth $0. They disappear from your portfolio. Nothing to redeem.

## Resolution timing

Markets resolve when the real-world outcome is confirmed by UMA's oracle. This can take anywhere from minutes to days after the event happens, depending on the market type.
