# Selling positions

Go to the Portfolio tab. Find your position. Hit the **Sell** button.

The system checks how much liquidity is available on the orderbook, then executes a sell at the best available price. Same Fill-And-Kill mechanism as buys.

You can also tell Oracle "sell my [market name] position" and it handles it from the chat.

<Warning>
  If there's low liquidity, you might not be able to sell your full position at once. The system will tell you how much it can fill.
</Warning>
