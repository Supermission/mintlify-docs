# Watchlist

Click the bookmark icon on any market card. It goes to your watchlist.

The Watchlist tab shows a stats card (how many you're watching, average volume, how many are ending soon, how many are trending) and your bookmarked markets below.

Sort by date added, volume, ending soon, or price change. Search within your watchlist.

## Price alerts

When a watched market moves more than 5% from your baseline, you get a notification. Different sound for up vs down. Click the notification to jump to that market.

<Tip>
  Use the watchlist to track markets you're interested in but not ready to trade. When the AI signals something on a watchlisted market, you already have context.
</Tip>
